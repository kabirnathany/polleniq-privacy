Frequently Asked Questions

Why is pollen data showing 0?
Pollen data depends on your location and the current season. Grass and weed pollen are typically 0 in winter and early spring. Tree pollen is most active March–May.

Why won't the app load pollen data?
Make sure you have an active internet connection. Enter your 5-digit US ZIP code on the setup screen. If the problem persists, close and reopen the app.

How do I reset the app?
Go to Settings → Clear All My Data. This will reset the app to its initial state.

Is my data private?
Yes. All symptom logs are stored only on your device and never uploaded to any server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

When does the Personal Risk Score unlock?
The risk score unlocks after you have logged symptoms on 7 different days.
